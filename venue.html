Where

We are getting married at the Little Green Wedding Barn near Diss in Suffolk. For more information about the venue please take a look at their website by following this link.

For a list of places to stay in the area visit the places to stay page on their website.

Taxis

The advice from our venue is that taxis are pre-booked no matter how short or long your journey is.

The venue does not recommend any particular taxi firm but suggests searching for "taxis Diss" to find local firms.

What time does the bar open?

1:45pm

The bar will be open from 1:45pm, so you are welcome to arrive anytime from then. There will be a 15-minute warning prior to the ceremony starting (at 3:00pm) as drinks are not allowed in the ceremony room.

What time does the venue close?

Last orders: 11:45pm

Last orders are at 11:45pm and the venue closes at midnight so this is the latest that taxis should be booked for.

Parking

There is parking onsite and cars can be left overnight at your own risk. The gate will be opened to collect them the next morning from 9am until the sun goes down that evening.

Payment Options at the Bar

The venue accepts both cash and card payments.
